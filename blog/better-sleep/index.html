Struggling to fall asleep? You’re not alone. Whether it’s anxiety, tension, or sleep apnea keeping you up, this 10-minute bedtime yoga routine offers a calming way to unwind and sleep faster—naturally. 🛌✨

🧘‍♀️ What This Bedtime Yoga Routine Includes:

This gentle and beginner-friendly yoga flow is designed to:

  • Release tension in your neck, shoulders, and lower back
  • Calm your nervous system
  • Improve blood circulation
  • Relieve stress and anxiety through mindful breathing

💫 Yoga Poses and Techniques Covered:

  • Neck Side Bending – Releases neck stiffness and eases mental fatigue
  • Shoulder Rolls – Loosens tight shoulders, ideal after a long day
  • Side Bending – Opens the side body and calms the nervous system
  • Number 4 Stretch & Pawanmuktasana – Targets hips, back, and relieves sciatica
  • Gentle Lumbar Stretch – Relieves lower back tension
  • Viparita Karani (Legs up the wall) – Soothes the mind, supports digestion, and improves blood flow
  • Bhramari Pranayama (Humming Bee Breath) – A deeply calming breathwork practice to reduce anxiety and promote sleep
  • Guided Shavasana – Final relaxation to guide you gently into restfulness

🌿 Who Should Try This?

This session is ideal for anyone looking for:

  • Yoga for sleep apnea, sleeplessness, or insomnia
  • Gentle yoga to reduce back or shoulder pain
  • Breathwork to relax the mind and body
  • A beginner-friendly night routine for better sleep

🛏 Why Practice Bedtime Yoga?

Scientific studies suggest that practicing yoga before bed helps regulate the nervous system, reduces cortisol levels, and improves melatonin production — all of which are crucial for quality sleep.
