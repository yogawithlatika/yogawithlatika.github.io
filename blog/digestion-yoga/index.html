🧘‍♀️ What This Yoga Session Offers:

This session combines gentle movements and mindful stretches to:

  • Stimulate digestive organs
  • Relieve bloating and constipation
  • Improve spine and hip flexibility
  • Promote a sense of calm and relaxation

💫 Yoga Poses and Techniques Covered:

  • Knee Bending Exercises – Increases knee flexibility and activates leg muscles
  • Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend) – Stretches the back, shoulders, and hamstrings while calming the mind
  • Bhunamanasana (Forward Stretch Pose) – Promotes spinal flexibility and gently massages abdominal organs

🌱 Benefits of This Practice:

  • Reduces stress and anxiety that impact digestion
  • Improves posture and flexibility
  • Alleviates tension in the back, hips, and legs
  • Enhances digestion and gut movement

📍For best results, practice this session on an empty stomach.
